Overview

Purpose

The purpose of my website is to promote my project and engage with potential customers.

My potential customers will be able to see captivating visuals, videos and stories to ignite the passion for adventure.

Plan their adventure, Meet the staff who will lead the adventure, see other customer reviews, booking and reservations.

Having multiple channels for customer support, contact and location information.

Audience

Our primary target audience consists of adventure enthusiasts and outdoor lovers who are seeking thrilling experiences in the great outdoors.

They are typically between the ages of 18 and 45, physically active, and have a moderate to high level of disposable income for recreational activities.

They often seek memorable experiences to break away from their daily routines and connect with nature.

They may struggle to find clear, detailed information about trip options, safety measures, and booking procedures.

Our website aims to meet these needs by providing a one-stop resource for all their white water rafting needs.

Our target audience accesses our website through mobile devices, such as smartphones and tablets, desktop or laptop computers

Therefore, our website is designed to be responsive, ensuring a seamless experience across all devices and screen sizes.
