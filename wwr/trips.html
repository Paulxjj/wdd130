Our Featured Rafting Trips

Los Dientes del Diablo

Experience the thrill of the "Devil's Teeth" rapids! This full-day adventure takes you through heart-pounding class IV and V rapids, surrounded by breathtaking canyon scenery. Includes lunch, safety gear, and expert guides. Perfect for adrenaline seekers!

Big Mallard

Our Big Mallard trip is ideal for families and first-timers. Enjoy a half-day journey down gentle class II and III rapids, with plenty of opportunities for swimming and wildlife spotting. All equipment provided. Fun and safe for all ages!

Half Day Adventure

Short on time? Our Half Day Adventure packs excitement into a few hours! Tackle a series of fun rapids, enjoy the beautiful river scenery, and make memories with friends or family. Morning and afternoon departures available.
